About Extensions Manager

Extensions Manager is a Google Chrome extension that allows you to reach fastly some Google Chrome's functionalities without going inside the several browser's menu options.

You can, for example, reach every extension you have installed in your browser and easily enable/disable or uninstall it, with just a click.
