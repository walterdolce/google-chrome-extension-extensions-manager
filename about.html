About Google Chrome Extension Manager

Google Chrome Extension Manager is a Google Chrome extension that allows you to reach fastly some Google Chrome's functionalities without going inside the several browser's menu options.

You can, for example, reach every extension you have installed in your browser and easily enable/disable or uninstall it, with just a click.

Google Chrome Extension Manager was developed by Walter Dolce. You can keep in touch with the author by sending an email to walterdolce@gmail.com or by visiting his blog, lifearoundweb.com.
